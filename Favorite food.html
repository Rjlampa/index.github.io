Crispy Fried Chicken

This dish is simply amazing.Eating fried chicken makes us feel young again.
The smell that makes us want to go home the crispyness that makes us smile.
The juiciness that makes us happy.
This dish reminds us of our childhood.

Ingredients

  • 1 Kilo of chicken
  • 1\2 Buttermilk
  • 3 Cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 Teaspoon of paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper>
  • Oil

Preperation

  1. Get the chicken and wash it thoroughly.
  2. Slice the chicken in the way you like it.
  3. Marinate the chicken pieces in the buttermilk.
  4. After marinating the chicken get a plastic bag or ziplock.
    Put the flour inside the bag and season the flour.
  5. Put the cicken inside the bag. Shake it until the flour paste to the chicken.
  6. Prepare the oil and the cooking pan that you will use.

Cooking Instruction

  1. Get the pan and put it in the stove.
  2. Put the oil and wait for the oil to get hot.
  3. when the oil is hot. Put the chicken by batches (Separate them by their sizes) so they can be cooked evenly.
  4. Wait for the chicken to get golden brown.
  5. When the chicken becomes golden brown. Remove them from the oil and let them rest for 5mins.
  6. Continue frying the other chickn.
  7. After frying the last batch of chicken. Get the first batch that you fried. Refry the to make it more crispier

Now prepare the dish with a nice plating and ENJOY EATING. :)
