Banana Split

Simple dish that can stisfy our craving for sweets.Its a dessert thats easy to do. A dessert that we dont usually eat.
Its a dessert that can makes us happy. Lift our mood for a day.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, halved lengthwise
  • 1/2 pint vanilla ice cream.(Preference)
  • Chocolate sauce, store-bought or homemade
  • Berries that you have.(Preference)

Preperation

  1. Get the ingredients and utensils needed.
  2. Wash the fruits

Instruction

  1. Cut the Banana in halves.
  2. Place the bananas in a large dish.
  3. Scoop 3 to 4 balls of ice cream onto the bananas.
  4. Top with the berries and chocolate sauce

ENJOY EATING AND BE HAPPY. :)
